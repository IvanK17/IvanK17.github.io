Microsoft

About Microsoft

Microsoft is one of the most valuable enterprises and the largest IT company in the world, generating over $125.8 billion revenue in 2019. Founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975, Microsoft Corporation, headquartered in Redmond, Washington, is one of the largest tech companies in the world.

What Microsoft do?

It develops, manufactures, licenses, supports and sells computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and services. Its best-known software products are the Microsoft Windows line of operating systems, the Microsoft Office suite, and the Internet Explorer, Edge web browsers, and Xbox. The company also produces a wide range of other consumer and enterprise software for desktops and servers.

What IT roles Microsoft can offer you?

There are plenty of professions at Microsoft such as Business Administrator, Hardware engineer, Corporate Technology Support, Reasercher, Sales Specialist and many many more.

What technologies do Microsoft IT's use?

The specialist working in Microsoft use a wide variety of software such as Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, Dynamics 365 and many more.

What is it like working in Microsoft?

Microsoft is a very open and inclusive company. It is a large organization and hard to know where to look for things at first, but once you have made a network of colleagues you will quickly gain more ground and accomplish more.
